# Quality Checklist

Run this checklist before final delivery.

## Evidence integrity

- Every market, segment, pain, WTP, size, conversion, retention, and behavior claim is marked as fact, hypothesis, or assumption.
- Unsupported model-derived claims are labeled `ГИПОТЕЗА`.
- External facts have sources; missing sources are stated as data gaps.
- Confidence levels are present for major conclusions.
- No invented statistics or fake citations appear.

## Pipeline completeness

- Discovery summary is present.
- Market research includes competitors, substitutes, alternatives, non-consumption, and channels.
- Segment discovery uses AJTBD and ABCDX.
- Jobs analysis includes main, related, functional, emotional, social jobs, desired outcomes, and switching forces.
- Value hypotheses and positioning are explicit.
- RAT prioritization is present.
- Validation plan includes experiment, cost, timeline, success/failure criteria, and decision rule.
- Customer Development plan is actionable.
- PRD/design/development sections are included when requested or implied.

## Handoff readiness

- Product team can see priorities and decision gates.
- Research team can run interviews/experiments.
- Designer can identify flows, screens, states, and UX constraints.
- Developer can identify requirements, dependencies, technical risks, and analytics events.
- Investor/client can understand opportunity, evidence, risks, and next actions.

## Final response quality

- Output is a single coherent Markdown document unless the user requested otherwise.
- Recommendations are tied to evidence and assumptions.
- Risks are not buried; they are summarized clearly.
- Next actions are concrete, sequenced, and time-bound.
