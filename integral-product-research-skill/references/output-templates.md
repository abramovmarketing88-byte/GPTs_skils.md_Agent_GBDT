## PRD add-on

Include enough detail for implementation without pretending unvalidated hypotheses are requirements. Use MoSCoW or RICE for prioritization.

Required PRD fields:

- Objective and success metrics.
- Users/segments and JTBD.
- Scope and non-scope.
- User stories with acceptance criteria.
- Functional requirements.
- Non-functional requirements.
- Analytics events.
- Dependencies and open questions.
- Release plan.

## Design requirements add-on

Include:

- UX principles.
- Primary user flows.
- Screen inventory.
- States: empty, loading, error, success, edge cases.
- Content/copy requirements.
- Accessibility requirements.
- Prototype testing tasks.
- Design handoff artifacts.

## Development requirements add-on

Include:

- System context and architecture assumptions.
- API/integration requirements.
- Data entities and lifecycle.
- Security/privacy requirements.
- Observability and analytics.
- Performance and reliability targets.
- Delivery plan and technical spikes.

## Investment memo add-on

Include:

- Thesis.
- Market opportunity with sourced facts only.
- Problem and target segment.
- Solution and differentiation.
- Business model hypotheses.
- Traction/evidence.
- Key risks and validation roadmap.
- Funding/use-of-funds assumptions.
- Decision recommendation.

## Commercial proposal add-on

Include:

- Client context.
- Diagnosed opportunity.
- Proposed scope.
- Deliverables.
- Timeline.
- Roles and responsibilities.
- Assumptions/exclusions.
- Pricing model placeholders if prices are unknown.
- Success metrics.
- Next step.
